Returns & Refunds

Last updated: 30 May 2026. This policy covers merchandise bought through hilltrek.co.za. It does not limit your statutory rights under South African law.

01 Who we are

Merchandise on this Website is sold by Hilltrek (Pty) Ltd, a private company registered in South Africa ("we", "us", "our"). Questions about an order or return go to info@hilltrek.co.za — please include your order number.

02 Faulty, damaged or incorrect items

If something is wrong with your order, we'll make it right. You may return an item for a repair, replacement, or full refund where the item is:

  • defective or of unacceptable quality;
  • damaged in transit and reported to us promptly;
  • not what you ordered (wrong product, colour, or size sent); or
  • missing parts or accessories.

For faulty, damaged, or incorrect items we cover the cost of return shipping and the replacement, or refund you in full including the original delivery fee. This is in line with your rights under the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008, including the implied warranty of quality that applies for six months after delivery.

03 Change-of-mind returns

We are a small Drakensberg outfit and do not offer change-of-mind returns or exchanges — for example, ordering the wrong size, or simply deciding you no longer want an item. Please check the size guide and product details carefully before you order, and reach out to us first if you're unsure.

This does not affect your rights for faulty, damaged, or incorrect goods set out in section 02, which we always honour.

04 How to claim a return

Email info@hilltrek.co.za within 7 days of delivery with:

  • your order number;
  • a short description of the problem; and
  • one or two clear photos of the item (and the packaging, if it arrived damaged).

We'll confirm the return and tell you where to send the item or arrange collection. Please keep the item in its original condition and packaging until the return is approved.

05 Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we'll let you know whether the refund is approved. Approved refunds are made to your original payment method, normally within 10 business days. Bank or gateway processing times may add a few days on top of that.

06 Your statutory rights

Nothing in this policy limits or replaces your rights under the Consumer Protection Act or any other applicable South African law. Where this policy and the law differ, the law applies.
