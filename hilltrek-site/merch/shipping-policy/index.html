Shipping

01 Where we ship

We currently deliver within South Africa only. We don't ship internationally yet. If you're outside South Africa and want something, email info@hilltrek.co.za and we'll see what's possible.

02 Delivery cost

Shipping is a flat R99 per order, anywhere in South Africa. The fee is shown at checkout before you pay, so there are no surprises.

03 Timeframes

Orders are usually delivered within 5 to 10 working days after your payment reflects. We dispatch via a national courier and aim to get your order moving within a couple of working days of the order being confirmed.

Deliveries to farms, smallholdings, and remote or outlying areas can take a little longer. Courier delays, public holidays, and load-shedding are outside our control, but we'll keep you posted if anything looks off.

04 Tracking & delivery

Where the courier provides tracking, we'll email it to you once your order is collected. Please make sure your delivery address and contact number are correct at checkout — re-delivery or redirection caused by an incorrect address may carry an extra courier fee.

05 Damaged or missing parcels

If your parcel arrives damaged, or doesn't arrive at all, tell us at info@hilltrek.co.za with your order number and we'll sort it out. Items damaged in transit are covered by our Returns & Refunds policy.

See also our Returns & Refunds policy, Legal Notice, and Privacy Policy.
