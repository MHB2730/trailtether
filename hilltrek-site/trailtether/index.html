// Built for the backcountry

Four tools. One screen.
Designed for the trail.

Every feature is engineered around the hard parts of hiking — losing signal, losing daylight, losing the group.

01 · Navigate

Offline topo maps that load before you leave the trailhead.

Download every contour line, switchback and water source before you go dark. Trailtether's vector maps render at sub-meter precision and stay readable in glove-on, headlamp-only conditions.

  • Pre-download entire regions for offline use
  • Live elevation profile + pacing as you climb
  • Built-in compass, bearing & waypoint nav
LIVE TRACK
02 · Stay together

See your whole group in real time — even with one device on signal.

Mesh-aware team tracking pins every hiker to the map. When one device picks up signal, the whole group's positions sync. Lose track of a friend? Their last-known location is always one tap away.

  • Live pins for up to 32 hikers per trip
  • Last-known-location relay over Bluetooth mesh
  • Quick check-in: SAFE · FOLLOW ME · ALERT
4 HIKERS ACTIVE
03 · Stay safe

One-tap SOS that broadcasts even when carriers don't.

Press and hold for 3 seconds. Trailtether packages your exact coordinates, altitude, vitals and route into a low-bandwidth burst — then keeps re-transmitting on every available channel until acknowledged.

  • Coordinate burst over cellular, satellite & mesh
  • Auto-notify emergency contacts with location pin
  • Safety check-in status: Safe · Alert · Danger
SOS READY
04 · Look back

Every hike, every metric — synced to your health apps.

Full elevation profiles, pace stability, cadence, calories. Your hike history syncs cleanly to Google Health Connect and Apple Health, so the climb counts toward everything else you train for.

  • Full elevation profile + ascent/descent breakdown
  • Heart rate, cadence, pace stability scoring
  • Health Connect & Apple Health sync
