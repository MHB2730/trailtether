// Who we are

A collective of explorers, conservationists and outdoor learners.

Hilltrek (Pty) Ltd was established by a collective of outdoor enthusiasts united by a deep appreciation for hiking, mountaineering, and the natural world. What began as a shared pastime among friends has evolved into a purpose-driven venture committed to fostering a vibrant community of explorers.

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and connect individuals through the power of authentic hiking experiences — curating route accounts, gear reviews, environmental insights and multimedia content that reflect the spirit and challenges of backcountry exploration across Southern Africa.

Whether you are a seasoned trekker or a first-time hiker, Hilltrek exists to support your journey — providing practical guidance, promoting responsible outdoor ethics, and celebrating the landscapes that make adventure possible. — Hilltrek (Pty) Ltd
