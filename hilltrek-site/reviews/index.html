// The Hilltrek Podcast

A record of progression — not expert-led instruction.

Hilltrek is a Drakensberg-based podcast following a group of friends who turned to the mountains as a way to step out of routine, reset, and test themselves in a real environment.

Each episode documents time spent in the Berg — planning routes, navigating escarpments, dealing with weather, fatigue and the limits of experience. The focus is practical: what worked, what failed, and what was learned on the ground.
