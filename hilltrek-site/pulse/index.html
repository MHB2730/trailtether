How privacy works here

Every number on this page is an aggregate. No individual hiker is named. No individual hike is published. Team names appear only when a team creator has explicitly opted in — and they can opt out from the Trailtether app at any time, removing their team from the next nightly refresh. The heatmap is built from track points binned into ~50m cells and only shows cells that at least three different hikers have crossed, so no individual route can be reconstructed.
