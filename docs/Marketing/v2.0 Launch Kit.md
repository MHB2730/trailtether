On the trail, the mobile app handles route planning, offline maps, GPS recording, live weather, hazard alerts, and a one-tap SOS. At home, base camp opens the desktop command centre to see exactly where you are, what the weather is doing on your route, and whether you're moving according to plan. Plan with the team. Track each other in real time. Log your activity to Google Health Connect. Trust that someone always knows where you are. Built in Cape Town by Hilltrek, free to use, available on Android and Windows.

## 8. Press / blog-post launch story (~450 words)

> # Trailtether 2.0: a hiker's situational-awareness platform now spans phone and PC
>
> *Cape Town, {{date}}.* Hilltrek today released **Trailtether 2.0**, the latest version of its free situational-awareness platform for hikers. The 2.0 milestone introduces a Windows desktop application that turns any PC into a base-camp dashboard, alongside the existing Android app that lives in the hiker's pocket.
>
> "Most hiking apps stop at the trailhead," said the founder of Hilltrek. "Trailtether starts there. The question we asked was: while someone is hiking the Berg, what does the person at home need to see? That became the desktop app — a live map, the weather over the route, the hazard reports, and a clear visual of whether the hike is going to plan."
>
> Trailtether pairs the hiker's mobile device with one or more PC clients via the user's Trailtether account. The mobile app records GPS, fetches weather, and broadcasts position; the desktop client renders all of it on a topographic or satellite map, complete with cave and shelter waypoints surveyed across the Drakensberg.
>
> **What's new in version 2.0**
>
> - **Windows desktop application.** Available as an MSIX installer with automatic silent updates.
> - **Google Health Connect integration.** Hikes log to Google Fit and any other Health Connect-aware app.
> - **Live mobile→PC tracking.** Real-time position broadcast to paired desktop clients.
> - **Polished home-screen layout** with prominent SOS and live-tracking actions.
> - **Self-hosted update channel** for users who prefer to install outside the app stores.
>
> The platform is free, ad-free, and offered without a premium tier. Hilltrek monetises through optional contracted services for hiking clubs, search-and-rescue teams, and conservation groups — not from end users.
>
> Trailtether was conceived after a series of incidents in the Drakensberg highlighted gaps in how hikers stay connected with people at home. South Africa's mountain ranges combine high altitudes, fast-changing weather, sparse cellular coverage, and a network of historic caves and shelters — conditions that demand a tool tailored to local terrain rather than a generic global hiking app.
>
> Trailtether 2.0 is available now:
> - Android — Google Play (rolling out) and direct download
> - Windows — trailtether.app and Microsoft Store (rolling out)
>
> For press enquiries, contact info@hilltrek.co.za.
