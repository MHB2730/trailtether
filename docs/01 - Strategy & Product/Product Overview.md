# Product Overview

Hilltrek is the parent outdoor brand. Trailtether is the hiking safety and situational-awareness app inside that brand.

Trailtether combines route planning, GPS recording, live team tracking, weather awareness, map intelligence, field tools, incident reporting, and base-camp monitoring. The product is built for real hiking conditions, especially South African terrain where weather, signal, safety, and route knowledge matter.

## Product Pillars

- Plan: route, terrain, weather, teams, checklist, itinerary.
- Navigate: 2D/3D maps, GPX, GPS, compass, trail intelligence.
- Track: recording, activity history, live team location, base-camp view.
- Protect: SOS, incident reporting, emergency contacts, off-trail alerting.
- Share: team chat, route notes, photos, post-hike debrief, community insights.
