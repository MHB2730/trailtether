# 🎯 Brand & Marketing Strategy

Trailtether's identity is built around preparation and safety, not just "adventure hype."

## 📍 Positioning
- **Core Message**: "Plan smarter. Hike safer. Stay connected on the trail."
- **Brand Voice**: Practical, prepared, and capable. Like an experienced hiking partner who knows the route and respects the mountain.

## 👥 Primary Audience
- **Day Hikers**: Reliable route and weather info.
- **Multi-day Hikers**: Offline maps, GPX, shelters, and emergency tools.
- **Hiking Groups**: Coordinated plans, team chat, and safety updates.
- **Local Communities**: Sharing trail intel and condition reports.

## 📢 Feature Pillars
1. **Plan**: Curated routes, elevation comparisons, GPX imports.
2. **Navigate**: 2D/3D map views, offline tiles, trail recording.
3. **Stay Aware**: Weather, cave/shelter data, community hazards.
4. **Coordinate**: Team invites, private chat, group alignment.
5. **Act Fast**: SOS tools, compass, flashlight, native emergency contacts.
