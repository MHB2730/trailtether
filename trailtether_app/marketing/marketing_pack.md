# Trailtether Marketing Pack

## Positioning

Trailtether is a hiking safety and planning app for serious trail days. It combines route discovery, offline-aware maps, live tracking, community field intel, team coordination, weather, GPX imports, cave and shelter data, and SOS tools in one app for Android and Windows.

## Core Message

Plan smarter. Hike safer. Stay connected on the trail.

## Primary Audience

- Day hikers who want reliable route and weather information before they leave.
- Multi-day hikers who need offline maps, GPX support, shelters, caves, and emergency tools.
- Hiking groups who coordinate routes, team plans, chat, check-ins, and safety updates.
- Local trail communities who share trail condition reports, incidents, reviews, and completed hikes.

## Taglines

- Plan smarter. Hike safer.
- Your trail plan, safety net, and crew hub.
- Built for hikers who do not wing it.
- Routes, weather, teams, SOS. One trail companion.
- The hiking app for prepared adventures.

## Elevator Pitch

Trailtether helps hikers prepare, navigate, and stay safer outdoors. Explore over 200 local trail routes, import GPX files, check weather, use offline-ready maps, record hikes, coordinate with teams, share trail intel, and keep SOS tools close when conditions change.

## Short Product Description

Trailtether is a trail planning and hiking safety app with route discovery, GPX imports, offline maps, live hike recording, team planning, weather, community intel, cave and shelter data, and SOS tools.

## Feature Pillars

- Plan: browse curated routes, review distance and elevation, attach GPX tracks, and schedule team hikes.
- Navigate: use 2D and 3D map views, offline tile support, GPS tracking, trail recording, and route history.
- Stay aware: view weather, cave and shelter details, community hazards, field intel, and trail reviews.
- Coordinate: create teams, invite hikers, chat, assign hike plans, and keep everyone aligned.
- Act fast: access SOS, emergency contacts, compass, flashlight, and field tools from inside the app.

## App Store Keywords

hiking, trails, trail planner, offline maps, GPX, GPS tracker, hiking safety, SOS, weather, hiking teams, route planner, mountain trails, trail reviews, outdoor navigation, hiking app

## Launch Announcement

Trailtether is now available for Android and Windows. Built for hikers who want more than a basic map, Trailtether brings route planning, GPX support, live tracking, offline-aware maps, team coordination, weather, community field reports, caves, shelters, and SOS tools into one focused app.

Whether you are planning a day route, preparing a multi-day mountain hike, importing your own GPX, checking trail conditions, or coordinating with a group, Trailtether keeps the important details close before and during the hike.
