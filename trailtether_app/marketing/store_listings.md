# Trailtether Store Listings

## Google Play Listing

App name: Trailtether

Short description:
Trail planning, GPX, offline maps, live tracking, teams, weather, and SOS tools.

Full description:
Trailtether is a hiking safety and trail planning app built for prepared hikers.

Explore local routes, import GPX tracks, check weather, view caves and shelters, record your hike, coordinate with teams, share field intel, and keep SOS tools close when conditions change.

Key features:
- Browse local trail routes with distance and elevation details.
- Import and manage GPX tracks.
- Use offline-aware map tools for route planning and navigation.
- Record hikes with GPS, distance, speed, elevation, and history.
- Switch between 2D and 3D map views.
- Check weather for key hiking locations.
- View caves, shelters, and linked hiking routes.
- Report hazards and field intel for the community.
- Create hiking teams, invite members, chat, and plan hikes.
- Access SOS, compass, flashlight, and safety tools.

Trailtether is for hikers who want to plan properly, move with better information, and keep their crew connected.

## Microsoft Store Listing

App name: Trailtether

Short description:
Desktop trail planning for routes, GPX files, weather, teams, and hike preparation.

Full description:
Trailtether for Windows gives hikers a larger planning surface before heading outdoors. Review routes, inspect trail details, manage GPX files, check weather, coordinate team plans, review community trail intel, and prepare safer hikes from your desktop.

Use Trailtether on Windows to plan your route, then take the Android app with you on the trail.

Key features:
- Route discovery and trail details.
- GPX import and route management.
- Weather and hiking condition planning.
- Team hike planning and community features.
- Cave, shelter, incident, and safety information.
- Hike history and recorded route review.

## App Category Recommendations

Primary category: Maps & Navigation

Secondary category: Health & Fitness or Travel & Local

Content notes:
The app includes location, community-generated safety reports, team chat, GPX file handling, and emergency/safety tools. Store privacy forms should reflect location usage, account/profile data, user content, and network services.
